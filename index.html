--- layout: default title: Jerome Faria - Composer & Sound Artist ---

Lisbon-based composer and sound artist working at the edges of experimental electronics, film scoring, and live performance.

Over two decades of work spanning ten albums across twelve labels, compositions for silent film and theatre, and performances alongside artists including Fennesz, Tim Hecker, Alva Noto, and Oneohtrix Point Never.

Latest

Overlapse XIII (2025)

Eight artists reimagine the 2012 album Overlapse for its thirteenth anniversary. Curator and mastering engineer. Contributors include Tren Go! Sound System, CAVERNANCIA, and João Vairinhos of LÖBO.

ALTAR (2024)

Debut release with NOx (duo with Pedro Roque of CAVERNANCIA). A 33-minute improvisation recorded near a nitric acid factory on Lisbon's Rua do Ácido Sulfúrico. Released on Colectivo Casa Amarela.

2504 (2024)

Released on the 50th anniversary of Portugal's Carnation Revolution. Duration: 25 minutes and 4 seconds—the revolutionary date written into its form. Drawing on Schaeffer, Radigue, and Reich to transform archival recordings into a meditation on inherited democracy.

“An almost romantic sound that recalls Eno's Discreet Music, with the additional creak of wind-up toys and waves of static that seem broadcast from the beach.”

— The Quietus
