## Selected Press

> "An almost romantic sound that recalls Eno's *Discreet Music*, with the additional creak of wind-up toys and waves of static that seem broadcast from the beach."
> — **The Quietus** (on Taylor Deupree collaboration)

> "Oscillated skillfully between electronic doom and subtle ambient. As if embodying two faces of the same coin—at times liberating, at times punishing, at times meditative, at times abrupt."
> — **Bodyspace** (on William Basinski support)

> "Atmospheric progressions... the allure of a waking dream."
> — **Indie Rock Mag**

> "It's almost as if we were contemplating the machine, forced to escape its synthesised perfection, about to stumble upon itself at any moment."
> — **Edição Limitada** (on Overlapse)

> "There is not a second to spare here, nor a moment to throw away."
> — **Ponto Alternativo** (on Overlapse)

> "Beautifully twisted chaos unfolding with no respite."
> — **The Devil's Mouth** (on ALTAR)

> "A last spurt of energy, a rise into the great unknown."
> — **A Closer Listen**

## Publications

**Glitch: Designing Imperfection**
Mark Batty Publisher, 2009

Edited by Iman Moradi, Ant Scott, Joe Gilmore, and Christopher Murphy. 144-page hardcover featuring over 200 glitch images and interviews with artists including Kim Cascone, Angela Lorenz, and Karl Klomp. Reviewed in Eye Magazine, Grafik, and Wallpaper*. A foundational text on glitch aesthetics.
